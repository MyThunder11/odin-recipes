Pizza

This margarita pizza is excellent trust me! ;)

Ingredients:

  • 1 (12 inch) pre-baked pizza crust
  • ½ cup pesto
  • 1 ripe tomato, chopped
  • ½ cup green bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 (2 ounce) can chopped black olives, drained
  • ½ small red onion, chopped
  • 1 (4 ounce) can artichoke hearts, drained and sliced
  • 1 cup crumbled feta cheese

Directions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).

  2. In a large bowl, combine the pesto, tomato, green pepper, olives, onion, artichoke hearts, and feta cheese.

  3. Place the pizza crust on a rimmed baking sheet. Spread the pesto mixture evenly over the pizza crust.

  4. Bake in the preheated oven until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly, about 15 minutes.

  5. Remove from oven and let cool for 10 minutes before serving.
